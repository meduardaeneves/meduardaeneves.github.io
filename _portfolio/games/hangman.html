This is a hangman game. Your goal is to guess the word chosen by the pc. The game is avaliable in engish and portuguese, before you start guessing you need to chose what language you want to play. For this game, exists 6 and 7 letters words, 500 units of each, for both languages. The exception is the group of 6 letters words in english, it contains 140 unis.

  • The portuguese words were extracted from the "Dicionário Online de Português"
  • The 6 letter words in english were extracted from the "Testbook"
  • The 7 letter words in english were extracted from the "BYJU'S website"
